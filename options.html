Side Menu

Enables or disables the navigation menu to the left of the site. Often, it gets in the way, so it's always good to know you can add and remove it whenever you want

USP Flash

Loads in a custom dynamic home screen packed with useful news, information and memes.

Select Theme

Changes the colours of the moodle pages to different colours. Some themes will stay out of your way and keep your eyes relaxed whereas others will burn your eyes out

College advertisements

Enables or disables the banner ads that the college leaves around the home page. They tend to clutter up the page so if you're fed up with poor layout, feel free to remove them

Background Video

Enables or disables your background video for the splashscreen. We know that the background video is hated by many so don't feel bad for removing it

Video on all pages

Enables you to have the background video work in the background of every page, no matter what you are looking at. Imagine the meme potential!

Mute Video Audio

Enables or disables the audio for the background video for when you want to listen to sick beats, or terribly memes...

Custom Youtube Video URL

Paste in a normal Youtube link and it will replace the background video with your very own custom creation, allowing you to truly make the site your own!
